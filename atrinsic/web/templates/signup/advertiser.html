How To Know If You're An Advertiser Or Publisher.

What is an advertiser?

An advertiser, also know as a merchant or retailer, is a Web site or company that sells a product or service online, accepts payments and fulfills orders. Advertisers partner with publishers to help promote their products and services.

What is an publisher?

A publisher, also known as an affiliate or reseller, is an independant party that promotes products and services of an advertiser in exchange for a commission on leads or sales. A publisher displays an advertiser's ads, text links, or product link on their Web site, in e-mail campaigns, or in search listings. Signing up as a publisher is a free service.
